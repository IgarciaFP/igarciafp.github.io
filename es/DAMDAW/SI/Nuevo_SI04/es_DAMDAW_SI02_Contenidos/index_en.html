SI02.- Software of a computer system.

Practical case

You already know the company BK Programación , in which our protagonists carry out their work as professionals in the IT sector. Let us remember the profile of each one.

Ada is the founder of the company and has extensive programming experience. Together with her, two senior computer technicians have started working at BK Programación: María , dedicated to the implementation of servers and maintaining web pages, and Juan , whose main job is to develop web applications and install Internet services.

On the other hand, we have Ana and Carlos , two young people who want to improve their professional training in the field of computing. Ana , who is passionate about graphic design, seems to choose to study the Multiplatform Computer Applications Development cycle, since it would do her very well to improve professionally. Carlos is thinking of studying the Web Application Development cycle, since it better fits his profile and he is curious to learn to program in web environments.

Ana has collaborated professionally on several occasions with BK Programación. He has done graphic retouching for some of the resources that appear on the websites that the company has created and maintains. Ana has commented to Carlos that she would like to do a company internship at BK Programming, since she could learn a lot and get more fully involved in web application development. Carlos thinks it is a good idea and decides to accompany Ana to go talk to the head of the company, Ada.
